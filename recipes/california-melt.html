California Veggie Melt

Description

A healthy, speedy recipe for those nights you don't have a lot of time but still want something warm, gooey and delcious!

Ingredients

  • 4 slices whole grain bread or english muffin, lightly toasted
  • 4 teaspoons Mayo
  • 1 tablespoon pesto
  • 1 avocado, sliced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/3 cup chopped almonds
  • 1 tomato sliced
  • 4 slices white cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven broiler.
  2. Mix mayo and pesto together in a small bowl.
  3. Lay toasted bread on baking sheet.
  4. Spread mayo mixture on top side of bread. Then add 1/4 avocado, mushrooms, almonds and tomatoes.
  5. Top with 1 slice of cheese.
  6. Boil until cheese melts and bubbles, serve warm.
