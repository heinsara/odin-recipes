Tempeh Gyros

Description

Seasoned tempeh meat with a luscious tzatziki sauce and crisp cool vegetables, all wrapped up in a warm pita. The Greek islands are calling and you'll want to taste them.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup veggie broth
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 2 tespoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • 1 and 1/2 teaspoons minced garlic
  • 1 (8 oz) package of tempeh
  • 4 (6 inch) whole wheat pitas
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 to 4 grinds of black pepper
  • 2 tomatoes sliced
  • 1/2 red onion, thinly sliced

Tzatziki

  • 2 small cucumbers
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon fresh dill
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 3 grinds of black pepper
