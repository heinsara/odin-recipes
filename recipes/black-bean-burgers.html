Homemade Black Bean Veggie Burgers

Description

Try this tasty, simple black bean burger recipe. They are easy to make and flavor can be customized with various herbs and spices. They batch well and are pretty healthy.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16oz) can of black beans
  • 1/2 green bell pepper, cut into 2 inch pieces
  • 1/2 onion, cut into wedges
  • 3 cloves garlic, peeled
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tablespoon chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of oil
  • Cooking spray

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high heat and lightly spray alumnium foil with cooking spray.
  2. Mash black beans with fork until thick paste forms.
  3. Heat oil in skillet on medium heat
  4. Finely chop bell peppers, onion and garlic in food processor
  5. Add peppers, onion and garlic to hot pan along with a pinch of salt, cook until softened and aromatic
  6. Stir cooled, cooked vegetables and beans together
  7. In a small bowl, whisk egg, chili powder and chili sauce and remaining salt
  8. Mix egg mixture into bean mixture
  9. Add breadcriumbs and mix until mixture holds together
  10. Divide into four patties and place on prepared foil.
  11. Grill about 8 minutes on each side
